Description

Bed: 1 double or 2 singular beds.
Capacity: up to 2 adults.
Location: first floor.
Dimensions: approximately 35 m².

The Oak Hotel Double room combines spaciousness, comfort, and style.

The soft and natural tones create a sense of coziness and tranquility in our guests. Each room has its own terrace revealing an incredible view of the unique Rhodope forests and meadows.

Your comfort and quality stay is of a top priority for us, so you choose the set-up which perfectly fits your needs- a common bedroom or twin beds, as well as, the option of adding an extra bed and a baby cot (upon request).
